Our Legacy of Excellence

Deolali High School was established in 1949 under the Saraswati Education Society, founded by Late Mr. P.H. Sidhwa and Late Mrs. T.P. Sidhwa. The society took over the "Zoroastrian Boarding High School" (ZBHS), pioneered by Mr. Jal Master during 1946‚Äì47, which was facing financial difficulties at the time.

The institution began with just 40 students and was registered as a public charitable trust. Qualified staff were appointed along with the competent Headmaster Late Mr. Santaram Varma. Within 2‚Äì3 years, the Government granted grant-in-aid to the school.

Through selfless service and sincere efforts, the school now serves approximately 3,500 students from Deolali Camp and surrounding villages.

Nurturing Every Child's Potential

At Deolali High School, we believe that love is the foundation of learning. Our approach revolves around one-to-one interaction between teachers and students, ensuring every child feels valued, heard, and inspired.

We encourage students to actively participate in class discussions rather than remain passive learners. Our educational philosophy emphasizes character development alongside academic excellence, with a "Love and Care" approach that has been our cornerstone since 1949.

The school management ensures a balanced team of experienced educators and energetic young teachers who focus on the all-round development of students. We strive to make learning enjoyable and meaningful.
